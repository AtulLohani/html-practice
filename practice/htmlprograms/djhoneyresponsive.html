ABOUT ME

Dj honey (atul lohani), is a well known dj from indore, dj honey fulfill a lifelong dream sharing his type of music to the world. He adds his own style to bollywood with a touch of hiphop and moombahton. He belives that melodic ryhthems enthralling bollywood vocals makes tracks great and his sets almost always start with hiphop, moombahton or deephouse. Leading to head bobbing. Fist pumping and pop edm's in mid bollywood session.

A Bollywood dj from indore. and a very known face for djing industry in indore. Starts djing from the age of 17 years. He enjoys his gigs from last 8 years in clubs and Events, private / corporate.
